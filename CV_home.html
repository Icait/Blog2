Introducing Your Guidebook: A space to diver deeper


Have you wondered about how our muscles bend? Why some of us have dry skin and not tohers? Ever marvelled at the invention of synthetic insulin?

Or something entirely different.

Did Stevens (Remains of the Day) ever really find dignity in his lifetime? What makes Renita D'Silva's books so nostalgic and heat wrenching yet well-balanced? What made J.D. Salinger come up with a character like Holden Caulfield?

Your Guidebook is a source that aims to answer all these perplexing, nagging questions in a detailed yet simple way.


Your Guidebook aim's to bring introspective and perceiving indivuduals to a common platform.
A platform to share ideas, thoughts, opinions, interpretations and insights.
And encourage the human trait of 'curiosity'.
